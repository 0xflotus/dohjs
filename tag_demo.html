Demo of Special DoHjs Tags

DoHjs provides special tags to load content (javascript, stylesheets, images) from a resolver of your choice. This allows circumnavigation of the user's default resolver and any filtering it may do.
Note that DoHjs only works on the DNS level and therefore content may still be blocked through other means such as a browser extension or IP-based filtering.
